Our exercise recommendations:

  1. Make sure to warm up and cool down before and after exercising, respectively.
  2. Do a variety of exercises like yoga, sports, and strength training so your whole body can reap the benefits.
  3. Give yourself a day or two to rest each week to avoid overusing or injuring your muscles.
  4. Once you start exercising, stick to a routine for the most benefit.
  5. Consult a doctor before starting an exercise that might not be suitable for you.
  6. Focus on your form and technique when exercising to ensure you are using your muscles properly.
