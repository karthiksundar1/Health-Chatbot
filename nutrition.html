Our nutrition recommendations:

  1. Eat a balance of the food groups including fruits, vegetables, dairy, meat, and grains.
  2. Control your portion sizes to avoid overeating.
  3. Try to make your diet consist of foods that consist of the rainbow colors.
  4. Try to cook your own food and control how frequently you eat from restaurants.
  5. Talk to your doctor before adopting a new diet.
  6. Lastly...It's ok to take break from healthy eating every once in a while.
